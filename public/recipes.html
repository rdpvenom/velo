Herb Roasted Chicken with Vegetables

Ad Space

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken or 4 chicken thighs
  • 3 carrots
  • 3 potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Chop the vegetables and place them on a baking tray.
  3. Season chicken with olive oil, herbs, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Arrange the chicken on top of or beside the vegetables.
  5. Bake until fully cooked and golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with pan juices or a light sauce.
